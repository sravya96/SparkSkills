One Startup from Every College

It is time to personalize education as one size does not fit all

In the global shift from the Industrial to the Digital Era, a new spirit is emerging in manufacturing,service and even agriculture. Instead of just learning to “follow instructions,” we need a generation of inquisitive, reflective and collaborative students who can work entrepreneurially and with responsibility for their own ventures and/or within existing organizations. It is the time we move beyond the mugging-up processes in education and build self-confidence, vision and drive into the next generation.
